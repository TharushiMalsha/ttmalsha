Vanila Ice Cream with Chocolate Chips

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup chocolate chips (semi-sweet or milk chocolate)
  • 1/2 cup toasted coconut flakes (optional, for a Sri Lankan twist)

cooking Instructions

Prepare Ice Cream Base:

  • In a mixing bowl, whisk together the heavy cream, whole milk, granulated sugar, and vanilla extract until the sugar is dissolved. This is your ice cream base.

Chill the Mixture:

  • Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate the mixture for at least 4 hours or overnight to enhance flavor and texture.

Churn in Ice Cream Maker:

  • Once thoroughly chilled, pour the mixture into your ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions, usually for about 20-25 minutes.

Add Chocolate Chips and Toasted Coconut:

  • In the last few minutes of churning, add the chocolate chips and toasted coconut flakes to the ice cream maker. Let it mix until they are evenly distributed.

Transfer to Container:

  • Transfer the churned ice cream to a lidded container.

Freeze:

  • Freeze the ice cream for at least 4 hours or until it reaches a firm consistency.

Serve and Enjoy:

  • Scoop the Vanilla Ice Cream with Chocolate Chips into bowls or cones and savor the delightful combination of creamy vanilla, rich chocolate chips, and the unique flavor of toasted coconut.
