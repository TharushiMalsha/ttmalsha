Chicken Pasta

Ingredients

  • 250g pasta (penne, fusilli, or your choice)
  • 300g boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, grated
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Lime wedges for serving

Cooking Instructions

Cook Pasta:

  • Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

Prepare Chicken:

  • In a pan, heat vegetable oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions, minced garlic, grated ginger, and green chili. Saut√© until the onions are translucent.

Add Chicken:

  • Add the chicken pieces to the pan. Cook until the chicken is browned on all sides.

Season with Spices:

  • Sprinkle curry powder, turmeric powder, chili powder, ground black pepper, and salt over the chicken. Mix well to coat the chicken in the spices.

Add Tomatoes and Curry Leaves:

  • Add chopped tomatoes and curry leaves to the pan. Cook until the tomatoes are soft and the mixture becomes somewhat saucy.

Pour in Coconut Milk:

  • Pour in coconut milk and stir well. Let it simmer for a few minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.

Combine with Pasta:

  • Add the cooked pasta to the pan and toss everything together, ensuring the pasta is well-coated in the flavorful sauce.

Check Seasoning:

  • Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed. Add more salt, pepper, or chili powder according to your preference.

Garnish and Serve

  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Serve the Sri Lankan Chicken Pasta hot, with lime wedges on the side.
