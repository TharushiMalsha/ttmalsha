Chocolate Cupcake

Ingredients

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt or buttermilk

For the Chocolate Ganache:

  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup semi-sweet or dark chocolate, finely chopped

Cooking Instructions

For the Cupcakes

Preheat Oven:

  • Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C). Line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.

Sift Dry Ingredients:

  • In a bowl, sift together the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

Cream Butter and Sugar:

  • In a separate large bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.

Add Eggs and Vanilla:

  • Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

Alternate Adding Dry Ingredients and Yogurt:

  • Gradually add the sifted dry ingredients to the wet ingredients in three parts, alternating with the yogurt. Begin and end with the dry ingredients. Mix until just combined; do not overmix.

Fill Cupcake Liners:

  • Spoon the batter into the cupcake liners, filling each about 2/3 full.

Bake:

  • Bake in the preheated oven for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean. Allow the cupcakes to cool completely before frosting.

For the Chocolate Ganache:

Heat Heavy Cream:

  • In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream over medium heat until it just begins to simmer.

Pour over Chocolate:

  • Place the finely chopped chocolate in a heatproof bowl. Pour the hot cream over the chocolate and let it sit for a minute.

Stir until Smooth:

  • Stir the chocolate and cream together until the chocolate is completely melted and the ganache is smooth.

Cool Ganache:

  • Let the ganache cool to room temperature. It will thicken as it cools.

Frost Cupcakes:

  • Once the cupcakes are completely cooled, spread or drizzle the chocolate ganache over the cupcakes.
