Chicken Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (about 4 pounds), giblets removed
  • 10 cups water
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups egg noodles or your preferred pasta
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Cooking Instructions

Prepare the Chicken:

  • Rinse the whole chicken under cold water and place it in a large soup pot.
  • Add 10 cups of water to the pot, ensuring the chicken is fully submerged.
  • Bring the water to a boil over medium-high heat.

Skim the Surface:

  • As the water starts boiling, you may notice foam rising to the surface. Skim off this foam using a ladle or spoon. This helps to achieve a clearer broth.

Add Vegetables and Seasonings:

  • Add diced onion, sliced carrots, sliced celery, minced garlic, bay leaf, dried thyme, salt, and pepper to the pot.
  • Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let it simmer for about 1 to 1.5 hours, or until the chicken is cooked through and the flavors have melded.

Remove Chicken and Shred:

  • Carefully remove the whole chicken from the pot and place it on a cutting board.
  • Once it's cool enough to handle, shred the chicken into bite-sized pieces using forks.

Cook Noodles:

  • While shredding the chicken, bring the soup back to a simmer and add the egg noodles or your preferred pasta. Cook according to the package instructions until the noodles are tender.

Return Shredded Chicken:

  • Add the shredded chicken back into the pot. Adjust the seasoning to taste, adding more salt and pepper if needed.
