French Fries

Ingredients

  • 4 large russet potatoes, peeled and cut into even matchsticks
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: additional seasonings like paprika, garlic powder, or your preferred spices

Cooking Instructoions

Prepare Potatoes:

  • Peel the potatoes and cut them into even matchsticks, about 1/4 inch thick. Rinse the cut potatoes under cold water to remove excess starch.

Soak Potatoes

  • Place the cut potatoes in a large bowl of cold water. Let them soak for at least 30 minutes (you can leave them in the water for up to 24 hours in the refrigerator).

Drain and Cool:

  • Use a slotted spoon or tongs to remove the partially cooked fries from the oil. Place them on a paper towel-lined tray to drain and cool for at least 15 minutes.

  • Serve the crispy French fries hot and enjoy!
