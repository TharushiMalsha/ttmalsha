Chicken Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked jasmine or basmati rice (preferably a day old and chilled)
  • 1 cup cooked chicken breast, diced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn, and diced bell peppers)
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: Sriracha sauce for extra heat

Cooking Instructions

Prepare Ingredients:

  • Ensure that all your ingredients are prepped and ready to go. Having everything ready makes the cooking process quick and efficient.

Scramble Eggs:

  • Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat. Pour the beaten eggs into the pan and scramble them until just cooked. Remove the scrambled eggs from the pan and set them aside.

Cook Chicken:

  • In the same pan, add another tablespoon of vegetable oil. Add diced chicken and cook until it's no longer pink in the center. Season with salt and pepper. Once cooked, remove the chicken and set it aside.

Saut√© Vegetables:

  • Add the remaining tablespoon of oil to the pan. Stir in minced garlic and grated ginger. Add the mixed vegetables and cook until they are tender but still crisp.

Combine Ingredients:

  • Add the chilled, cooked rice to the pan. Break up any clumps and mix well with the vegetables.

Add Chicken and Eggs:

  • Return the cooked chicken and scrambled eggs to the pan. Stir everything together until well combined.

Season with Sauces:

  • Pour soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil over the rice mixture. Stir well to ensure even distribution of the sauces. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper to taste.

Add Green Onions:

  • Toss in chopped green onions and continue to stir-fry for an additional minute or two.

Optional Heat:

  • If you like a bit of heat, you can add Sriracha sauce to taste.

Serve Hot:

  • Once everything is heated through and well mixed, remove the pan from heat. Serve the Chicken Fried Rice hot.
