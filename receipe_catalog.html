Main

Chicken Kottu

Chicken Kottu is a popular and iconic street food dish in Sri Lanka, celebrated for its unique preparation and delicious flavors. Commonly found in bustling street corners and local eateries, Chicken Kottu has become a beloved part of Sri Lankan culinary culture.

Cheese Kottu

Cheese Kottu, a delectable variation of the traditional Kottu, has become a beloved and flavorful street food option in Sri Lanka. Known for its creamy texture and cheesy goodness, Cheese Kottu adds a delightful twist to the iconic dish, making it a popular choice among locals and tourists alike.

Egg Kottu

Egg Kottu is a popular and flavorful street food dish in Sri Lanka that offers a delicious twist on the traditional Kottu. Recognized for its simplicity, affordability, and satisfying taste, Egg Kottu has become a favorite among locals and visitors alike.

Chicken Fried Rice

Chicken Fried Rice in Sri Lanka is a delicious and popular dish that reflects the country's diverse culinary influences. Combining fragrant Basmati or local rice with tender chicken and an array of flavorful spices, Sri Lankan Chicken Fried Rice is a satisfying and hearty option enjoyed by locals and visitors alike.

Seafood Rice

Seafood rice, often known as seafood paella or seafood fried rice, is a delectable dish that brings together the flavors of various seafood and aromatic rice. While there are numerous variations globally, the description provided here is inspired by the Spanish paella.

Chicken pasta

Chicken Pasta in Sri Lanka is a delightful fusion dish that combines the beloved flavors of Italian pasta with Sri Lankan spices and culinary influences. This unique creation has gained popularity for its comforting and flavorful appeal, making it a favorite among locals and providing a distinct twist to traditional pasta dishes.
